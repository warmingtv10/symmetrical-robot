✦ Destination Overview

Why Visit Panchagarh?

Tucked into the very tip of Bangladesh, Panchagarh is the country's northernmost district — a land of misty mornings, tea-carpeted valleys, ancient stone forts, and the singular gift of watching the world's third-highest peak rise from the plains.

🏔

Kanchenjunga Views

On clear winter mornings from November through February, the snow-capped peak of Kanchenjunga (8,586 m) is visible from Tetulia Dak Bungalow.

🌿

Tea Gardens

Northern Bangladesh's sprawling tea estates create a patchwork of emerald green across the landscape.

🪨

Stone Rivers

The Mahananda, Karatoa, and Talma rivers carry countless coloured stones, supporting a unique stone-collection economy.

🌐

Bangalbandha Land Port

Bangladesh's only land port connecting to India, Nepal, and Bhutan simultaneously.

🏛

Ancient Heritage

Bitorgarh Fort — Bangladesh's largest archaeological site — along with ancient temples dot the landscape.

How to Get There

✈️

বিমান রুট, সময়সূচি ও ভাড়া

নিকটতম: Saidpur Airport (SPD) — Panchagarh থেকে ~৯০ কিমি

রুটএয়ারলাইনসময়ফ্লাইটভাড়া (BDT)

* আনুমানিক ভাড়া। Saidpur → Panchagarh ~১.৫ ঘণ্টা।

গেটওয়ে: Hazrat Shahjalal Intl Airport (DAC), Dhaka

রুটএয়ারলাইনসময়স্টপভাড়া (USD)

* Economy class আনুমানিক ভাড়া।

🚌 স্থলপথে

  • Dhaka → Panchagarh: ৮–১০ ঘণ্টা
  • Hanif, Shyamoli, Nabil Express
  • Panchagarh Express ট্রেন
  • Bogura থেকে: ৩ ঘণ্টা

🏨 Where to Stay

  • Panchagarh Circuit House
  • Tetulia Dak Bungalow
  • Hotel Sugandha
  • DC Rest House

🍽 Local Food

  • Panchagarh mishti
  • River fish curry
  • Bharta-bhat
  • Fresh local tea

📅 Best Time

  • Nov–Feb: Kanchenjunga views
  • October: Tea picking
  • May: 🎣 মাছ ধরার মৌসুম
  • Avoid: July–Aug floods

💡 Travel Tips

  • Book Dak Bungalow early
  • Carry warm layers (8–12°C)
  • Hire guide for Bitorgarh
  • Carry cash — few ATMs

📞 Emergency

  • Tourist Police: 01769-660099
  • DC Office: 0568-61033
  • Fire Service: 199
  • Police: 999
